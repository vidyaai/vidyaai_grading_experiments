Microdissection:-
" The use of microscope 
for dissection is called #
microdissection . "
Types
1) Chromosome microdissection :-
In this technique 
use a fine glass needle under
microscope to remove a portion
from a complete chromosome . "
2) Laser microdissection :-
Use of a laser under 
microscope to dissect selected cell . "
3) Laser capture microdissection :-
" use of a laser
under microscope to cause selected
cell to adhere to a film ."
