Staining :-
Meaning :-
Staining means colourization
or dye .
Definition :-
Staining is the process in 
which cell or tissue is colourized with
particular stain ( chemical solution or
dye ) . for identification or # study
Important of Staining :-
most of biological structure ( organelles ,
cell , microorganism ) are transparent , so
the staining technique is used to
obtain contrast ( difference ) between 
different structures of a cell .
Types of Staining :-
(1) Vital Stain :-
Stain that can be
applied on living cell's with killing them .
